About Wisdom creative School

Established in 1998, Wisdom creative School is a premier educational institution committed to fostering a culture of lifelong learning, integrity, and service. Our dedicated faculty and modern infrastructure empower students to excel academically and socially.

We believe in nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and character development alongside academic excellence. Our innovative teaching methods and state-of-the-art facilities create an environment where every student can discover their unique potential.

🎯

Our Mission

At Wisdom Creative School, our mission is to nurture a generation that embodies the perfect blend of academic excellence, ethical values, and life skills essential for personal growth and societal contribution. We are committed to fostering an educational environment that encourages intellectual development while shaping moral and ethical values rooted in character and integrity.

We strive to provide students with the tools and knowledge they need to excel academically and socially. Our aim is to build responsible global citizens with compassion and the ability to make meaningful contributions to their communities. By creating a supportive atmosphere for holistic development, we inspire students to become lifelong learners and positive changemakers.

🔮

Our Vision

Our vision at Wisdom Creative School is to become a model institution recognized for its ability to integrate modern education with strong ethical principles. We aim to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and moral compass required to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

We envision a community of learners who excel academically, think critically, and act responsibly with a strong sense of purpose and humility. Our school aspires to set a benchmark in quality education by fostering creativity, innovation, and personal integrity among students, preparing them to lead meaningful and successful lives.

Admission Process

  1. Registration: Collect the admission form from the school office or download it from our website.
  2. Submission: Submit the completed form along with required documents (birth certificate, previous school report card, Aadhaar card, and passport-size photographs).
  3. Assessment: Students from Grade 1 onwards will undergo a basic assessment in English, Mathematics, and Science.
  4. Interview: Parents and students will meet with the school principal for an informal discussion.
  5. Admission Confirmation: Selected candidates will receive an admission confirmation slip. Parents must complete the fee payment process within the stipulated time to secure the seat.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery & Kindergarten: Minimum age as per government norms (3+ years for Nursery, 4+ years for LKG, and 5+ years for UKG by March 31st of the admission year).
  • Grade 1 and Above: Submission of the previous school’s report card and transfer certificate.
  • Islamic Education: Students must be willing to participate in Islamic studies and moral education as part of the curriculum.

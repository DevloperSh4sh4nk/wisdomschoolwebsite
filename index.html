About Wisdom Group of Schools

Established in 1998, Wisdom Creative School is a premier educational institution committed to fostering a culture of lifelong learning, integrity, and service. Our dedicated faculty and modern infrastructure empower students to excel academically and socially.

We believe in nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and character development alongside academic excellence. Our innovative teaching methods and state-of-the-art facilities create an environment where every student can discover their unique potential.

Our Mission

At Wisdom Group of Schools, our mission is to nurture a generation that embodies the perfect blend of academic excellence, ethical values, and life skills essential for personal growth and societal contribution.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a model institution recognized for its ability to integrate modern education with strong ethical principles. We aim to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and moral compass required to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

Our Values

Our educational philosophy and community culture are guided by five core principles: Excellence, Integrity, Creativity, Respect, and Service.

Curriculum Overview

At Wisdom Group of Schools, we provide a well-rounded curriculum that integrates modern education with strong Islamic values. Our program follows national education standards while nurturing moral and spiritual growth.

Examination Policy

Our evaluation system ensures a fair and continuous assessment of students' growth:

  • Formative Assessments — periodic exams and class tests
  • Summative Assessments — mid-term and final exams
  • Regular Quran & Islamic Studies Assessments
  • Parent Feedback — progress reports and discussions

Subjects Offered

A balanced mix of modern academics and Islamic education to support both scholastic achievement and spiritual growth.

Library & Laboratories

Our campus provides modern learning spaces to enhance knowledge and discovery:

  • Library — A well-stocked collection covering academics, Islamic literature, hadith, and inspirational biographies.
  • Science Labs — Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs for hands-on learning.
  • Computer Lab — Modern systems for coding, programming, and digital literacy.

Admission Process

  1. Registration: Collect the admission form from the school office or download it from our website.
  2. Submission: Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  3. Assessment: Students from Grade 1 onwards will undergo a basic assessment.
  4. Interview: Parents and students will meet with the school principal.
  5. Admission Confirmation: Selected candidates will receive an admission confirmation slip.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery & Kindergarten: Minimum age as per government norms
  • Grade 1 and Above: Submission of the previous school's report card and transfer certificate.
  • Islamic Education: Students must be willing to participate in Islamic studies and moral education.
